Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is a vibrant and joyous festival celebrated by millions of people across the world, especially in India. It signifies the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil. During Diwali, homes and streets are adorned with colorful lights, diyas (oil lamps), and rangoli (decorative designs made with colored powders or rice). Families come together to exchange gifts, share delicious sweets and snacks, and offer prayers to the goddess Lakshmi for prosperity and wealth. The festival also includes the bursting of fireworks, creating a dazzling spectacle in the night sky. Diwali brings people of different cultures and backgrounds together, fostering a sense of unity and happiness that truly illuminates the spirit of the occasion.
