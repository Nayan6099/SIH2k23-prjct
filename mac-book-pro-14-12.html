Taj Mahal
The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most iconic and magnificent structures in the world. Built in the 17th century by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, this white marble mausoleum is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Its exquisite beauty lies in its intricate carvings, graceful domes, and the mesmerizing play of light on its pristine white surface. The Taj Mahal is a symbol of eternal love and has captivated visitors with its timeless charm for centuries, making it a symbol of India's rich history and architectural brilliance.
